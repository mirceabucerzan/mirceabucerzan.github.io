About Me

Passionate Android Engineer with 6+ years of experience in the software industry, working on applications that have a positive impact on people's health and fitness.

Strong knowledge of the Android SDK, Kotlin and Java programming languages.

Being able to create amazing experiences for devices that can fit in one's pocket is the main reason I have decided to become a mobile developer. The freedom and options that the Android platform offers, both from a user and software engineering perspective, made it an obvious choice.

Focused on clean and high quality code, with a strong drive for professional success, I take pride in my work, whether it's on the job or while working on personal projects. I enjoy breaking down and implementing complex features, following good software design principles, overcoming obstacles along the way, while making sure everything is delivered on time.

Throughout my career, I have been part of cross-functional teams and have had experience collaborating with people that specialize in other areas such as iOS, web, backend, UX, QA and so on.
